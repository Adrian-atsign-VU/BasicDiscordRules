Article II: Conduct Towards Others

  1. No harassment, bullying, or personal attacks against others will be tolerated.
  2. Members must respect others' privacy and not share personal information without consent.
  3. Do not share personal information about our teacher, engage in bullying, or spread falsehoods about them.
  4. Memes about classmates and teachers are permitted, provided they are shared in good taste.
  5. Sharing or promoting illegal activities or content is forbidden.

Article III: Behavior and Enforcement

  1. Language should be appropriate, avoiding excessive use of offensive or disruptive language.
  2. Report any suspicious or harmful behavior to server helpers or admins immediately.
  3. Impersonation of other members, including classmates or administrators, is not allowed.
  4. Former students must be placed in a separate chat group.
  5. False information about due dates or events provided by non-helper/admin members may lead to restricted access to channels, chats, or calls.

Article V: Rights and Responsibilities

  1. All members are entitled to freedom of expression (free speech) but must recognize that this is a private Discord server.

  3. The Good Samaritan Rule applies: Admins and helpers may exercise discretion in rule enforcement, provided they act in good faith.

Article VI: Modifications and Helper Roles

  1. Modifications to this constitution may only be made if at least three admins or helpers agree to the change.
  2. One classmate will be assigned to oversee changes to ensure that no helper or admin has abused the The Cyber Security Discord Rules Constitutional Document during modifications. This classmate does not have decision-making power but is responsible for observing and reporting any misuse to other classmates.
  3. Classmates may only assume a helper role if they have assisted students for more than three weeks and have been vouched for by another admin or helper.
  4. Helper roles can only be revoked by admins.
  5. If helpers do not help others or share information or bully others, they will be removed from their helper role.

Definitions

Admins
Members with the highest level of authority on the server. They oversee all operations, enforce rules, and have the final say in disputes and major decisions.
Helpers
Members who assist with moderating the server and supporting other classmates with work, due dates etc. They enforce rules under the guidance of admins and help maintain order.
